Nimble, a Wolfram|Alpha® menubar client for macOS.

Facts only...

Get facts and other info from Wolfram|Alpha instantly. Use Nimble to solve complex math problems, convert between units, and get facts on people, history, and culture.

...and then some.

Wolfram|Alpha knows a lot more than maths and culture. How much wood could a woodchuck chuck if a woodchuck could chuck wood? Nimble knows.

“The menubar client is every bit as intuitive as the web interface, meaning it’s easy to use...”

“Nimble can handle pretty much anything you’d normally throw at Wolfram Alpha...”
